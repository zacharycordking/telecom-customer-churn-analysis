# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze telecom customer behavior and identify the customer characteristics most associated with churn in order to support retention strategy.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Questions
- What is the overall churn rate?
- Do churned customers have lower tenure?
- Are monthly charges associated with churn?
- Do churned customers have lower total charges?
- Does contract type influence churn?

## Key Findings
- Customers who churned generally had lower tenure.
- Customers who churned tended to have higher monthly charges.
- Customers who churned had lower total charges overall.
- Month-to-month contracts showed the highest churn rates.

## Recommendations
- Focus retention efforts on newer customers.
- Review pricing and value for higher monthly charge customers.
- Encourage customers to move to longer-term contracts.

## Dataset
Source: Kaggle Telecom Customer Dataset
